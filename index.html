About

Who am I?

I’m Taku, a UX/UI Designer and Front-End Developer based in Canada with 10 years of experience. I specialize in helping businesses build innovative digital products, guiding the process from UX research and UI design to prototyping and integration.

Design Philosophy

These are a set of principles that I adhere by as a Desginer

Be wrong

It took me a long time to learn this, but as a designer, my first iteration should be wrong. I should be open to feedback and criticism to find the best solution.

Understand the process

Good design is not just beautiful but also functional. I always try to understand what is the purpose of my design, who is this for, and what problems does this solve.

Keep it stupid simple

Anyone who’s worked in sales will be familiar with this phrase. It is also applicable to design. It’s important to make sure the work I do add’s value, not complexity.

Have fun

I know this sounds extremely corny, but it’s easy to forget to have fun. After dozen’s of iterations the task may start to feel heavy. I try to take each piece off feedback as a challenge to do something better.
